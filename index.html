End of Semester Reminder

Dear Professor _______,
The semester is over and summer is here! There are materials on reserve for your course(s) that we believe belong to you. Unless these should be placed on a reserve list for a course taught this coming summer, they should not remain in the reserves room.
Please respond with how you would like to get your materials back from this semester. We can send them through campus mail or put them aside for you to pick up at the library. If you would like to pick up your materials, please let us know so we can have them processed for you ahead of time. Alternatively, if you want your books sent through campus mail, please provide us with your on campus address. Otherwise, if you are planning on using them for a summer semester course, please respond with which course they will be for and if they should be marked as 2hr, 24hr, or 72hr reserve books.

Professor-Owned Books Left Behind

Dear Professor _______,
We hope your semester is going well. We noticed that we have your personal book from a previous semester in our Reserve room. The book[s] we have is/are ____________________________. We are happy to hold on to it/them if you would like to place it/them on reserve in the future. If not, however, we can return it/them to you via campus mail.
If we do not hear from you in two weeks, we will donate this/these item[s]. Please let us know what you prefer, or if you have any questions.
Have a lovely day.
